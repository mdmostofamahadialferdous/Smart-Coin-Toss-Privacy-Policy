Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Smart Coin Toss (“the App”) handles user information. This app is developed as an offline utility application and does not collect or transmit personal data.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. No user accounts, logins, or backend servers are used.

Custom Images and Names

The App allows users to select custom images from their device gallery and set custom names for coin toss options. All selected images and text data remain strictly on the user’s device and are stored locally using device storage only.

Permissions

The App may request access to media files solely for selecting images for customization. This permission is used only for app functionality and is never used to collect or transmit data.

Third-Party Services

The App may display ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous usage data as described in Google’s privacy policy.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Internet Usage

The core functionality of this App works completely offline. An internet connection is not required, except for loading advertisements if available.

Children’s Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
